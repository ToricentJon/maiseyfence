About Maisey Fence

A Family Legacy Built on Trust

Founded by a second-generation fencer, Maisey Fence was built on a legacy of craftsmanship passed down through family. That hands-on expertise is at the heart of everything we do — from the materials we choose to the care we put into every installation.

We're committed to Alaska and to growing an exceptional company for our community. Customer satisfaction isn't just a goal — it's what drives us every single day. We take deep pride in our work, and it shows in every fence we build, every repair we make, and every handshake at the end of a job well done.

We're always looking to grow and expand the services we offer, because our community deserves a fencing company that evolves with their needs. From permafrost to extreme weather, we build fences designed to withstand whatever nature throws at them — and we're just getting started.
