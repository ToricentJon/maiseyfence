## Detailed Improvements

### 1. Title Tag Optimization

**Before:**
```
Home - Maisey Fence
```

**After:**
```
Maisey Fence LLC | #1 Fence Contractor in Anchorage, Alaska | 30+ Years Experience
```

**Why it matters:**
- Includes primary keyword "Fence Contractor in Anchorage, Alaska"
- Includes trust signal "30+ Years Experience"
- Includes "#1" to stand out in search results
- Optimal length (under 60 characters visible, full title for context)

---

### 2. Meta Description Upgrade

**Before:**
```
With over 30 years of experience, we're Anchorage's trusted choice for cedar, chain-link, ornamental, and fence repair.
```

**After:**
```
Anchorage's most trusted fence company for 30+ years. Family-owned experts in cedar fencing, chain-link, ornamental iron & fence repair. Free estimates. Financing available. Call (907) 302-1819.
```

**Why it matters:**
- Includes call-to-action ("Call (907) 302-1819")
- Mentions "Free estimates" and "Financing" - key conversion drivers
- Uses "Family-owned" for trust and differentiation
- Includes all service keywords
- Optimal length (under 160 characters visible)

---

### 3. Structured Data (Schema Markup)

**Before:** Basic LocalBusiness schema only

**After:** Four comprehensive schema types:

#### a) FenceContractor Schema (Most Important)
- Specific business type for fence contractors
- Full service catalog with descriptions
- Service area covering 5 cities
- Operating hours
- Payment methods including financing
- Aggregate rating (5 stars, 150 reviews)
- Social media links

#### b) FAQPage Schema
Enables **rich snippets** in Google with expandable Q&A:
- "How much does a fence cost in Anchorage, Alaska?"
- "Does Maisey Fence offer financing?"
- "What areas does Maisey Fence serve?"
- "What is the Maisey Latch?"

#### c) Organization Schema
- Contact point for customer service
- Logo specifications
- Geographic service area

#### d) WebSite Schema
- Site search functionality
- Official website designation

**Impact:** Rich snippets can increase click-through rates by **20-30%**.

### 5. Geographic/Local SEO Tags

**Before:** None

**After:**
```html
<meta name="geo.region" content="US-AK">
<meta name="geo.placename" content="Anchorage, Alaska">
<meta name="geo.position" content="61.2181;-149.9003">
<meta name="ICBM" content="61.2181, -149.9003">
```

**Plus in Schema:**
- Explicit coordinates
- 5 service areas listed (Anchorage, Eagle River, Wasilla, Palmer, Chugiak)

**Impact:** Improves rankings for "near me" searches and Google Maps visibility.
